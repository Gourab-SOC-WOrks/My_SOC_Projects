### Investigation Workflow

1. Collect raw email data (.eml, headers)
2. Analyze email headers and authentication results
3. Perform content and social engineering analysis
4. Extract and analyze URLs and domains
5. Assign risk level and final verdict
6. Recommend containment and remediation actions

---

### Key Learning Outcomes

* Understanding of email authentication mechanisms
* Ability to identify phishing despite authentication success
* Practical SOC-style documentation
* Incident response decision-making

---

### Disclaimer

This project is conducted strictly for **educational and training purposes**. All artifacts, URLs, and scan results are simulated to demonstrate SOC analysis techniques. No real phishing infrastructure was deployed or interacted with.
